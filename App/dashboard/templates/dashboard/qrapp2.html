MBcheck Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-09-03 • Last updated: 2025-09-03

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy for MBcheck (the QR code verification application) explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our services, including our website at uniqbo.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two categories of information from users:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII): name, email address, telephone number, postal address, billing details, and any other data you provide.
  • Non-Personal Information: device type, operating system, IP address, timestamps of use, and QR codes used (including hashed or tokenized identifiers where applicable).

3. Use of Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • To personalize your experience and respond to inquiries.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app’s functionality and security.
  • To send service updates, announcements, and—where permitted—promotions and news via email.
  • To perform analytics and usage statistics (in aggregate where feasible).
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Permissions are used strictly to enable application features; we do not sell personal data.

4. Disclosure of Data

We do not disclose personal data except:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • When required by law, court order, or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of MBcheck, our users, or the public.
  • With trusted service providers under confidentiality and data processing agreements.
  • When requested by competent authorities, in accordance with due process.

5. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards—such as strong passwords, encryption in transit and at rest where applicable, and firewalls—to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Users’ Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Rectify inaccuracies or update information.
  • Request deletion (erasure) of personal data.
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain situations.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Minors

MBcheck is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.

8. International Transfers

If personal data is transferred across borders, we take appropriate measures to ensure adequate protection consistent with applicable data protection laws.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. We then securely delete or anonymize the data.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through in-app notices, email, or other appropriate channels. The “Effective date” above reflects when the current version took effect.

11. Contact

For questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at admin@uniqbo.com or call +591 78889899.
