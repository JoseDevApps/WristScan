Qr Pass Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy for Qr Pass (the QR code verification application) elucidates the manner in which we collect, utilize, disclose, and safeguard information when you employ our services, encompassing our website at uniqbo.com.

2. Information We Collect

We gather two categories of information from users:

  • Personally Identifiable Information: name, email address, telephone number, postal address, billing details, and any other data you furnish.
  • Non-Personal Information: device type, operating system, IP address, usage date/time, and QR codes used.

3. Use of Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • To personalize your experience and respond to inquiries.
  • To provide and enhance the app’s functionality.
  • To send updates, promotions, and news via email.
  • To analyze usage statistically.
  • To comply with laws and regulations.

Permissions are used solely for application functionality without commercial intent.

4. Disclosure of Data

We do not disclose personal data unless:

  • With explicit user consent.
  • Required by law or legal process.
  • To protect rights or property of the application or users.
  • Shared with trusted providers under confidentiality agreements.
  • Requested by competent authorities.

5. Security

We implement passwords, encryption, and firewalls to safeguard personal data.

6. Users’ Rights

Users may:

  • Access their data.
  • Rectify inaccuracies.
  • Request deletion.
  • Object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent.

7. Minors

Our application is not intended for individuals under 13. We delete any such data if discovered.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date and, if significant, users will be notified.
